# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

Business Sales Performance Analytics is a Data Analytics project focused on analyzing sales transactions to uncover meaningful insights about revenue, profit, products, countries, and business performance.

The project uses data analysis and visualization techniques to identify top-performing products, profitable markets, loss-generating products, and business expenses. Based on the findings, strategic recommendations are provided to help improve profitability and support business growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top revenue-generating products
- Find the most profitable countries
- Detect loss-making products and regions
- Evaluate business expenses affecting profitability
- Generate actionable business recommendations

## 📈 Key Insights

### Top Revenue-Generating Products
Some products generated significantly higher revenue and should remain a major focus area for the business.

Examples:
- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER

### Most Profitable Countries
The highest profits were generated from:
- United Kingdom (UK)
- Netherlands
- EIRE
- Germany

### Low-Performing Countries
Certain countries contributed very little profit or generated losses, including:
- Saudi Arabia
- Bahrain
- Czech Republic

### Loss-Making Products
Some products negatively impacted overall profitability.

Examples:
- WHITE CHERRY LIGHTS
- CREAM SWEETHEART MAGAZINE RACK
- WOODEN BOX ADVENT CALENDAR

### Business Expenses
Certain expenses reduced overall profits, including:
- AMAZON FEE
- Manual Adjustments
- Bad Debt Adjustments
- CRUK Commission
- Bank Charges

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the business should:

✅ Focus on promoting top-performing products.

✅ Increase marketing efforts for products that are close to becoming top sellers.

✅ Expand operations in highly profitable countries.

✅ Reassess business operations in loss-making regions.

✅ Remove or redesign products generating consistent losses.

✅ Reduce unnecessary operational expenses to improve profit margins.
